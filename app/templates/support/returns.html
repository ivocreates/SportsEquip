How to Return an Item

Online Returns
  1. Log into your account
  2. Go to "My Orders"
  3. Find your order and click "Return Item"
  4. Select return reason
  5. Print the return label
  6. Pack the item and attach the label
  7. Drop off at any courier location
Customer Service Returns
  1. Call our customer service: +91 1800-123-4567
  2. Provide your order number
  3. Explain the return reason
  4. Receive return authorization
  5. Pack the item securely
  6. Schedule a pickup or drop off
  7. Track your return status

Return Conditions

✅ Returnable Items
  • Unused items in original condition
  • Items with original tags attached
  • Items in original packaging
  • Defective or damaged items
  • Wrong items received
  • Items not as described
❌ Non-Returnable Items
  • Used or worn items
  • Items without original tags
  • Personalized or customized items
  • Swimwear and undergarments
  • Items damaged by customer
  • Gift cards and vouchers

Refund Information

Refund Timeline
  • Processing: 2-3 business days after we receive your return

Refund Methods
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method
