Help Center

To place an order:

  1. Browse our products and add items to your cart
  2. Click on the cart icon to review your items
  3. Click "Proceed to Checkout"
  4. Fill in your delivery information
  5. Choose your payment method and complete the order

You can track your order by:

  • Logging into your account and visiting "My Orders"
  • Using the order tracking number sent to your email
  • Contacting our customer support team

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
  • Debit Cards
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Net Banking
  • Cash on Delivery (for select locations)

Creating an account is easy:

  1. Click "Register" in the top navigation
  2. Fill in your details (name, email, password)
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Start shopping!

Benefits of having an account:

  • Faster checkout
  • Order tracking
  • Wishlist functionality
  • Exclusive offers and discounts
