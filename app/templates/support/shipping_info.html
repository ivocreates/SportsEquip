Shipping Policy

Order Processing
  • Orders are processed Monday through Friday
  • Orders placed before 2 PM are processed the same day
  • Orders placed after 2 PM or on weekends are processed the next business day
  • You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships
Packaging
  • All items are carefully packed to prevent damage
  • Fragile items receive extra protective packaging
  • We use eco-friendly packaging materials whenever possible
  • Discreet packaging available upon request
Delivery Requirements
  • Someone must be present to receive the package
  • Valid ID may be required for delivery
  • Signature confirmation required for high-value items
  • Delivery attempts: 3 times before return to warehouse

Special Circumstances

Delayed Deliveries

Deliveries may be delayed due to:

  • Weather conditions
  • Natural disasters
  • Government restrictions
  • High demand periods (festivals, sales)
Undeliverable Packages

If a package cannot be delivered:

  • We'll attempt delivery 3 times
  • SMS and email notifications will be sent
  • Package will be held at local facility for 7 days
  • After 7 days, package returns to our warehouse
  • Return shipping charges may apply for re-delivery
